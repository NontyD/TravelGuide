Monthly Calendar

January February March April May June July August September October November December

January

January is the height of summer in South Africa, with warm and sunny weather. It is a great time for beach holidays and exploring the natural beauty of the country.

February
Cape Winelands Harvest Festivals

February continues with the summer season, offering warm temperatures and plenty of outdoor activities. It is a perfect month for wildlife viewing in national parks.

March
Cape Town Carnival
Cape Town International Jazz Festival

March brings the end of summer and the beginning of autumn. The weather remains warm and it is an excellent time for visiting vineyards and experiencing harvest season.

April
Two Oceans Marathon

April is autumn in South Africa, with cooler temperatures and fewer tourists. It is a great time for hiking and exploring the countryside.

May

May brings cooler temperatures and is an ideal time for safari trips as the vegetation is less dense, making it easier to spot wildlife.

June
Knysna Oyster Festival

June marks the beginning of winter in South Africa. It is a great time for whale watching along the coast and enjoying the quieter off-season.

July
Durban July

July is winter in South Africa, with cooler temperatures and fewer crowds. It is a good time for visiting indoor attractions and experiencing the country's rich culture.

August
Oppikoppi Music Festival

August continues the winter season, with mild temperatures and fewer tourists. It is a great time for exploring the cities and enjoying indoor activities.

September
Hermanus Whale Festival

September is heritage month in South Africa. Different people of South Africa dress differently to celebrate their heritage. It is also springtime, making it ideal for visiting gardens and nature reserves.

October

October is spring in South Africa, with blooming flowers and warmer temperatures. It is a great time for outdoor activities and exploring the natural beauty of the country.

November
Ficksburg Cherry Festival

November brings warmer temperatures and is a great time for beach holidays and outdoor adventures. It is also a good time for bird watching as many migratory birds arrive.

December

December is the start of summer in South Africa, with warm weather and festive celebrations. It is a popular time for tourists, with many events and activities taking place.
